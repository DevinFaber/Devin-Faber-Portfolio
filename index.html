Intro

Hi, my name is Devin Faber-Milks, I am 21 years old, and I currently live in a small town called Rigby which is located in the south-eastern section of the state of Idaho.

I am originally from Colorado Springs, Colorado although I have lived in Idaho for two years now after moving from Colorado back in August of 2020.

While I like living in Idaho, Colorado is home to me as I have lived there most of my life, and the change of scenery when I moved to Idaho was drastically different to the landscape Colorado had, especially with the mountains being so close to the city of Colorado Springs. The area where I live in Idaho is very flat and is primarily used for farming which really contrasts with the landscape of Colorado Springs.

About Me

I chose the path of software engineering for a multitude of reasons, but the primary reason being I was not sure what I wanted to go to college for, I completed two years of college to get most of the prerequisite courses out of the way but I was unsure of what I wanted to pursue for a degree major. A friend of mine suggested I give software engineering a try, as I have never done anything related to it, and I joined the Thinkful program as it was the same program my friend had gone through.

Outside of software engineering I have many interests and hobbies some of which include military aviation, cars and motorcycles, car racing, as well as gaming with my friends and spending time with my family.
